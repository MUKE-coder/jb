Speaker Introduction Bio
Muke Johnbaptist, affectionately known as JB, is a tech entrepreneur and educator who is passionate about empowering young people to succeed in the digital economy.
JB is the Founder and CEO of Desishub Technologies, a registered tech company in Uganda that builds websites, mobile apps, and smart business solutions for companies across Africa and beyond.
What makes JB's story truly inspiring is that he is entirely self-taught. Starting from scratch with no formal computer science degree, he learned to code on his own and turned that skill into a thriving business. Today, he has taught over 400 students through his online academy and coding bootcamps, and his YouTube channel has reached over 14,000 subscribers worldwide.
As a digital entrepreneur, JB has earned over $18,000 selling online courses and digital products, proving that with the right skills and determination, young Africans can build profitable businesses in the tech space.
JB is known for his ability to explain complex technology in simple terms and for his gift of inspiring others to take action on their dreams. His message is clear: anyone can learn, anyone can build, and anyone can succeed.
