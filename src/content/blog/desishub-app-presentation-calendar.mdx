## 📋 PRESENTATION GUIDELINES

### Preparation Checklist

- ✅ App must be functional and demo-ready
- ✅ Presentation deck prepared (15-20 minutes)
- ✅ Live demo prepared with test data
- ✅ Category alignment clearly demonstrated
- ✅ Technical documentation ready
- ✅ Q&A preparation

### Presentation Format

- **Duration:** 15-20 minutes per team
- **Structure:**
  - Introduction (2 min)
  - Problem & Solution (3 min)
  - Live Demo (10 min)
  - Technical Overview (3 min)
  - Q&A (5 min)

### Attendance

- All team members must be present
- Stakeholders invited for all presentations
- Friday sessions: 2 teams (Teams 1 & 2)
- Saturday sessions: 2 teams (Teams 3 & 4)
